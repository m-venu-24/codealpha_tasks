# Task 1 - Robotics in Healthcare

## Project Overview

This project presents a detailed research report on the applications of robotics in healthcare. The report explores how robotic systems are transforming modern medicine through surgical assistance, rehabilitation support, hospital automation, and intelligent healthcare solutions.

## Objectives

* Understand the role of robotics in healthcare.
* Study surgical and rehabilitation robots.
* Analyze benefits and challenges of healthcare robotics.
* Explore future developments in medical robotics.

## Topics Covered

* Introduction to Robotics
* Robotics in Healthcare
* Surgical Robots
* Rehabilitation Robots
* Hospital Service Robots
* Benefits and Challenges
* Future Scope

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Word
* Research Journals
* Academic References
* PDF Documentation

## Results

The study demonstrates that robotics improves surgical precision, patient safety, treatment quality, and operational efficiency in healthcare environments.

## Conclusion

Healthcare robotics is transforming the medical industry by providing intelligent, accurate, and efficient solutions that improve patient outcomes and support healthcare professionals.
