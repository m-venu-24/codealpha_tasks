# Task 4 - Role of AI in Autonomous Robots

## Project Overview

This mini-project explores the importance of Artificial Intelligence in autonomous robotic systems. The report explains how AI technologies enable robots to perceive their surroundings, make decisions, learn from data, and operate independently.

## Objectives

* Understand Artificial Intelligence concepts.
* Study autonomous robotic systems.
* Explore AI technologies used in robotics.
* Analyze real-world applications of AI-powered robots.

## AI Technologies Covered

* Machine Learning
* Deep Learning
* Computer Vision
* Natural Language Processing
* Sensor Fusion

## Applications

* Self-Driving Vehicles
* Delivery Robots
* Industrial Automation
* Healthcare Robotics
* Agricultural Robots
* Drones

## Diagrams Included

* AI Robot Architecture
* Autonomous Decision and Execution Flow

## Results

The project demonstrates how AI significantly improves robot intelligence, adaptability, and autonomous operation.

## Conclusion

Artificial Intelligence is the core technology driving the development of modern autonomous robots across various industries.
